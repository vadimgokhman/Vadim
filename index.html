Life in Progress

It's never too late to change direction

I was born in a small town named Nikolaev in Ukraine on September 9, 1981. While I was studying at school I've trained in athletic sports, and really enjoyed it. That’s why I’ve chose to enter the Physical Culture High School and went in for professional sports (graduated in 1999)

At the age of 19 I moved from Ukraine to Israel in hope for Better Life . In 2002 I was drafted into the Israeli army, that helped me much to integrate into a new environment and opened up a lot of opportunities. I was demobilized from the army in 2004 in rank of First Division Sergeant. Military service in a combat battalion made my will strong and I’ve learned to stay calm under pressure in any critical situation.

In search of my first “adult” work and Financial Stability I’ve got a job at Arava Border Crossing, and I’m working there till present day. I’ve started in a professional security service and moved to management position as Duty Manager. In my job I have to provide computer skills, different development projects demand system knowledge. In my current position, solving problems, looking for flaws and finding alternatives to achieve the goal – is the main occupation.

But I want to Achieve More… I want to try myself in different spheres Progressive and Challenging.

That’s why at the age of 39 I’ve decided to make a big step forward and to start a new paragraph in my life.

And now I am on QA courses ... Studying QA Testing and Improving my Skills ...
